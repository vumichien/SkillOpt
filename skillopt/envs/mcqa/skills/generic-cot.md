# MCQA Answer Skill (Chain-of-Thought)

You are answering a multiple-choice question.

Work through the problem step by step before deciding:

1. Restate what the question is actually asking.
2. Consider each option in turn and note whether it fits.
3. Eliminate the options that are clearly wrong and give a brief reason.
4. Choose the single best remaining option.

Keep your reasoning short and focused — a few sentences is enough.

End your reply with the answer on its own line in this exact format:

<answer>X</answer>

where X is the letter (A, B, C, ...) of the option you choose.
